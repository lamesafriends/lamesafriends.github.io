Donating books to La Mesa Community Library

We accept new and "gently-used" books.  Donated materials may be added to the library's collection.
Materials not added will be sold in the Bookstore and the proceeds benefit La Mesa Library. Unfortunately, we cannot accept the following:

· VHS tapes, audio cassettes, software
· Magazines, including National Geographic
· Sets/series (encyclopedias, Reader's Digest novels, etc.)
· Old almanacs and yearbooks
· Textbooks
· Soiled, mildewed, very worn and/or marked-up books (underlined/highlighted)

Thank you in advance for observing these guidelines in your donations to the library.
