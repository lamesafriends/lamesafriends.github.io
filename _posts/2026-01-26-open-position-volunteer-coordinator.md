**Volunteer Coordinator, Friends of the La Mesa Library**

**Non-Profit  Description**
The Friends of the La Mesa Library is a nonprofit organization dedicated to fostering a strong, modern library system in the La Mesa community. Established in February 1969, it operates as a 501(c)(3) with a mission to promote, support, and preserve library services and to meet community needs. The organization focuses on education, advocacy, and communication with local libraries and residents. Passionate volunteers drive its initiatives, helping to ensure access to library resources and programs for everyone in the community.

**Role Description**
This is a **flexible volunteer role** for a Non-Profit Volunteer Coordinator based in La Mesa, CA, with the opportunity to perform some tasks remotely. The Volunteer Coordinator will oversee recruitment, training, and management of volunteers, ensuring they are effectively matched with roles that support Friends of La Mesa Library initiatives. Regular responsibilities include organizing volunteer schedules, coordinating community outreach activities, and serving as the primary point of communication between prospective and active volunteers and the Friends of the La Mesa Library. This volunteer role also entails fostering a positive and collaborative environment for all participants.

**Wish List**

* Prior experience in coordinating people & projects
* Experience with Gmail, Google Drive, Google Docs, Google Sheets experience preferred
* Experience in training people to be set up for success
* Live in La Mesa, California or active part of community
* Proactive communicator and a “people person”
* Loves organization and project management
* Ability to match volunteer skillsets with open projects & opportunities
* A passion for libraries and being involved in the community

We recognize that most people will not meet every qualification. If this volunteer role excites you, we would love for you to apply.
